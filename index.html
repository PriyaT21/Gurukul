Welcome to G.K. Gurukul

GK Gurukul embarked on its academic journey in 2017 and dedicated itself towards the objective of creating global citizens with an international perspective. GKG is a child centered co-educational, Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) affiliated school. Besides academic excellence, the School focuses on encouraging the students to utilize their abilities and develop strong ethical values of integrity, politeness, kindness and respect for all.

We at GKG firmly believe that the students need to appreciate their own art, music, literature,history, as well as their own environment, scientific and spiritual theory.

Primary School

We believe learning should be interesting and practical. Almost every subject has Kits – Maths, Science, Robotics, etc. We intend to draw out and nurture their natural abilities, be it artistic, scientific or linguistic. We strongly emphasize holistic growth with subjects like Public Speaking, Creative Writing, Theatre, Music, Dance, and numerous internal and external competitions.
